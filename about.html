Kendra Higgins

Founder | kendra@kaeatelier.com

"Experiences where passion and purpose come together."

Born and raised in Mid-Michigan farm country, Kendra currently resides along Michigan's golden coast. Self-motivated she started in the design industry at an early age following her passion through internships, college, and the cubicle life. It was those early years that defined her passion for the underdog, the self-starter, the ones with potential yet to be found - and so Kae Atelier (k-ae atuh-lay) was born to support non-profits and small businesses throughout Michigan.
